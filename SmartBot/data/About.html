Product

  • Automate your curtain with the help of UI interface.
  • Practical and useful for high ceiling curtains, elderly, and disabled people.

Features

  • Low cost
  • Easy to use
  • No installation overhead
  • Power efficient

Instructions

  1. Enter your SSID and password in the settings menu to connect to your network
  2. After successfull connection you can control the curtains from the home page
  3. Move the slider to open/close the curtains

Components

  • ESP-32 module
  • Motor
  • Frictionless Roller
  • Battery
  • Battery holder
